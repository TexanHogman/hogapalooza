About

Hogapalooza is a summer event hosted by Rick and Jess Hogge

Welcome

Rick, Jess, Nick, Ben and Ella are excited to host a day of music, food and swimming. Join us for the day, or bring your RV and spend the weekend. While the party starts Saturday at noon, nearby attractions include Balcones Canyonlands for a great hike and Hidden Falls Adventure Park for motorized off-road fun. Come early and enjoy the area.

We've had lots of fun during past events and hope to continue the tradition this year celebrating our country's independence with family and friends. We will provide Smokey Mo's BBQ with 'fixins' and sides. Of course we'll be riding dirt bikes so if interested bring long pants and closed toes shoes. To minimize dust, this crazy fun will be limited to off band times and away from the stage and pool area.

At nightfall, it's fireworks time so bring your favorite and let's light up the sky!
