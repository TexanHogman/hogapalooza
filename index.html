About

Hogapalooza is a summer event hosted by Rick and Jess Hogge

Welcome

Rick, and the kids are excited to host a day of music, food, swimming and camping. Join us for the day, or bring your RV and spend the weekend. While the party starts Saturday at noon, nearby attractions include Balcones Canyonlands for a great hike and Hidden Falls Adventure Park for motorized off-road fun. Come early and enjoy the area.

Hogapalooza 2012 was a big success but we learned a few things, so this year Will Boldt and Troy Boldwyn have offered to cook on-site. Thanks guys!! Additionally, to minimize dust, dirt bikes will be limited to off band times and away from the stage and pool area.

Do

  • Bring your swimsuit, towel and sunscreen
  • Bring chairs
  • Tip the bands
  • Be responsible
  • Mark your calendar and please RSVP

Don't

  • Bring your pet
  • Bring your firearms
  • Miss It
  • Forget your family

The Bands

Serenada Hill

Georgetown Texas

noon - 1:30pm


Serves up an eclectic blend of Americana spiced with a steady diet of originals. You are likely to hear anything from The Decemberists to Gene Autry. Michael Hare, previously of Blue Rail plays rhythm and lead guitar, harmony and lead vocals. Michael's wife, Julie Hare sings harmony and lead, adds in some bluesy harmonica, some percussion and an occasional mandolin. Pat McHenry sings harmony and lead vocals, rhythm and lead guitar, and occasionally bass guitar. Together they make Serenada Hill a diverse, rootsy sounding experience.

zoom

Bentli

Austin Texas

2:00pm - 4:00pm


Born in El Paso Texas, and currently residing in Austin, Texas. BENTLI's musical journey began at an early age as he honed in on his grandfather's guitar-playing skills. Inspired by all facets of life- family, love, friends, and even death, BENTLI uses his life experience and his strong voice to create a sound that encompasses soulful, melodic rock with edgy, big harmonies which create a stew that can perhaps best be described as 'Texas-home grown'.

Bubba Coletrane and the Train Wrecks

Austin Texas

Horn infused rock band that specializes in having fun, energetic shows. Plays anything from funk to country, but mostly good old classic rock and blues.

Bill Me Later

Austin Texas

7:00 - 8:00pm


PayPal-Austin's house band 'Bill Me Later' has been playing together for nearly five years. They cover rock and blues from the 50s to the 2000-teens including the Beatles, Jimi Hendrix, the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Muddy Waters, the Clash, Iggy Pop, and the Black Keys. The current line-up consists of the prolific rhythm duo of Ed Kuhn and Bob Russin on bass and drums, Steve Sturtevant on lead guitar, and Dan Becker on vocals and guitar.
